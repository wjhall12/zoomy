Our Frequent Flyer Program

The Zoomy Frequent flyer program is convenient for our for passengers who often travel to and from Atlanta. And for customers who live and travel to U.S. cities like New Orleans, Miami, Charlotte, Dallas and Houston. By becoming a Zoomy Frequent flyer member, you'll earn miles based on the amount you pay for your flight with Zoomy Airlines.Those miles can be redeemed for free air travel or cashed in for cold hard cash. You will also earn double miles if you pay before 30 days of your flight.
